About

PyBass is a web interface for media storage and synchronization service which allows the user to store their music in cloud and synchronize across devices, also make their own playlist and sortlisted their favourite musics. It's a free service with ads free user interactive interface providing unlimited storageTM with the facilities of uploading their choice of music along with streaming and downloading.
